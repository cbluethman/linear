Sound Effects
=============

Place the following .wav files in this directory:

- click.wav    - Short click sound when grabbing a vector
- snap.wav     - Snap sound when releasing a vector
- whoosh.wav   - Whoosh sound for matrix transformation animations
- correct.wav  - Positive tone for correct quiz answers
- wrong.wav    - Error tone for wrong quiz answers
- navigate.wav - Subtle click for lesson navigation

These should be short (< 1 second), low-volume sound effects.
The app will work fine without them — SoundService fails silently.

Free sound effect sources:
- freesound.org
- mixkit.co/free-sound-effects
- soundbible.com
